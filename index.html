KH.Ahmad Dahlan

an Indonesian Muslim religious leader and revivalist

Early life

  • 1868 born in Yogyakarta, Netherland Indie
  • 1883 Darwis was sent to Mecca, to perform the pilgrimage, and deepen his religious knowledge. Due in part to financial assistance from his brother-in-law, Kyai Haji Soleh. The night prior to his departure, the community gathered at his father's house to pray for Darwis' safety during the pilgrimage.
  • 1909 KH Ahmad Dahlan joined the Budi Utomo organization and there he taught religion and the lessons needed by members
  • 1912 KH Ahmad Dahlan founded an organization called Muhammadiyah which was engaged in society and education
  • 1918 he founded the Aisyiyah organization for women. for the youth, Ahmad Dahlan formed Padvinder or Pandu (now Scouts) called Hizbul Wathan
  • 1923 at the age of 54, KH Ahmad Dahlan died in Yogyakarta. Then he was buried in the village of Karangkajen, Brontokusuman, Mergangsan, Yogyakarta
  • 1961 based on the Decree of the President of the Republic of Indonesia No. 657 of 1961 for his services, the state gave him the honorary title as a Hero of Indonesian National Independence.
